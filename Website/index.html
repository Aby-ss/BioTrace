BioTrace

!Image!

BioTrace is an integrated system of camera traps and sound sensors that regularly provides data input to an AI processing hub which can detect animals and record their numbers.

Challenge Information

Find a way to improve the tracking of biodiversity in the wetlands. This involves using technology such as camera traps to identify animals, particularly at night. Propose a small project by determining the appropriate technology to use, where to install it, and how many cameras are needed.

Able to identify animal based on their species.

Able to record and track populations of species in wetlands range over long period of time.

Easily understandable human interface.

Variety of sensors to ensure accurate tracking.

- Active Infrared Sensors

- Passive Infrared Sensors

- Stereo Cameras

- LiDAR Sensors

The drawbacks of being inable to track biodiversity include the loss of essential knowledge about certain species, difficulty in identifying endangered or threatened animals, and the loss of control of overpopulated species.
